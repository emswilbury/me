I love music. imagine a chain smoker, but with music. addicted to music. can't have a day without "smoking" a playlist. I'm an addict. I'm a nerd of music. if I ever lick an album, I would always know everything about each track of it. when listening to music, I don't go with the vibe nor lyrics first. I digest the composition, the arrangement. I sort the drums, I sort the guitar, I sort the bass, I sort the harmonising vocals. then followed by vibe, lyrics last. I think this is my way of listening because I was into producing songs before. thus my brain got its own default processing method to music. I never really cared about lyrics. no song ever carried my feeling away. I'm hardly get swayed of anything, but a song, The Beatles made. last ever song they'd release back in 2023. I cried over that song, even by the 5th time of listening to it. powerful.
